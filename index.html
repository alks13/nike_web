About Nike

Nike is a renowned and successful company in the sports apparel and footwear industry. Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, it later changed its name to Nike in 1971, inspired by the Greek goddess of victory. Nike's iconic "Swoosh" logo represents speed and motion. Nike has been a trailblazer in athletic footwear innovation, introducing groundbreaking technologies such as Nike Air and Nike Shox. These advancements revolutionized comfort and support in sports shoes. Collaborating with famous athletes like Michael Jordan, LeBron James, and Cristiano Ronaldo, Nike created exclusive collections and signature footwear lines, elevating its brand status and desirability. Beyond product excellence, Nike engages in social and environmental initiatives. It strives to improve working conditions and minimize environmental impact through various programs. Today, Nike remains a leader in the sports industry, offering a diverse range of products for athletes and active individuals worldwide. With a commitment to innovation and athlete partnerships, Nike continues to empower individuals to reach their full athletic potential.
